## Overview

Using the sample studies as a model, you will design and run your own study to gain insight into an aspect of your LLM use that matters to you. The topic and method are yours to choose, guided by themes from the course.

Building on the methods introduced in the reflection study, you will design and run a second, more structured study of your own LLM use. This study bridges the sample studies and your final project, giving you practice with a full research cycle.

## Steps

1. Write a research plan describing (week 4): 
    1. your research question and how it builds on the diary study findings, 
    1. your methods, what data you will collect, and how you will analyze it
    1. a plan for what is the role of LLMs in how you will conduct the study and an explanation of the reasoning behind your plan (ideally based in diary study findings)
2. Collect data according to your plan.
3. Analyze data according to your plan.
4. Report on your findings in a deck, brief text summary, and record a presentation.

## What to Submit
1. Study design document (week 4-5)
2. Final findings summary, deck, and recorded presentation (week 10)

## Gen AI Guidelines

Plan how you will use LLMs in the study before you start it, and explain your reasoning in the study plan.
